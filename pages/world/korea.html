LG Innotek to suspend production at Pyeongtaek motor plant LG Innotek, the electronic component manufacturing unit of LG Group, will suspend the production of its vehicle motor plant in Pyeongtaek, Gyeonggi Province, later this year to relocate facilities to other sites at home and abroad, according to industry sources Friday.
Samsung aims to adopt AI in 100m Galaxy phones SAN JOSE, California – Samsung Electronics will carry out software updates for its Galaxy AI to be featured in 100 million Galaxy smartphones this year, the company’s mobile business chief said Wednesday.
K bank resumes IPO process, eyes 2024 listing The internet-only K bank said Friday that it is pushing for an initial public offering this year following a failed attempt last year. “The IPO will be an opportunity for K bank to take another big leap,” said CEO Choi Woo-hyung, who took the post on Jan. 1. “K bank will grow with all its members through thorough preparations.”
Super Junior to meet global fans on Asia tour Legendary boy band Super Junior will hold an Asia tour. The group, celebrating 19 year since its debut, will hold an Asia concert tour titled "2024 Super Junior Super Show Spin-off: Halftime." The tour begins with a concert in Seoul in June, according to the band's agency SM Entertainment.
